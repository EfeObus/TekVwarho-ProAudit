## Tier 1: ProAudit Core

### Pricing
- **Monthly:** ₦25,000 - ₦75,000/month
- **Annual:** 15% discount (2 months free)
- **Annual Range:** ₦255,000 - ₦765,000/year

### Target Market
- Small businesses in Nigeria
- Sole practitioners
- Startups
- Freelancers with basic bookkeeping needs
- POS business owners

### Included Modules

#### General Ledger & Chart of Accounts
- Standard chart of accounts templates
- Custom account creation
- Account hierarchy management
- Account types: Asset, Liability, Equity, Revenue, Expense

#### Journal Entry Management
- Manual journal entries
- Recurring entries (up to 10 templates)
- Entry reversal
- Basic approval workflow (single approver)

#### Basic Financial Reporting
- Trial Balance
- Income Statement (P&L)
- Balance Sheet
- Cash Flow Statement (indirect method)
- Export to PDF/Excel

#### Tax Engine (Basic)
- Standard tax rate configuration
- Tax calculation on transactions
- Basic tax reports
- Single jurisdiction support

#### Customer & Vendor Management
- Contact information
- Transaction history
- Basic aging reports
- Up to 500 customers/vendors

#### Basic Invoicing
- Invoice creation and sending
- Up to 100 invoices/month
- Basic templates (3 designs)
- Payment tracking

#### Standard Audit Logs
- User activity logging
- 90-day retention
- Basic search/filter
- Non-WORM (editable for corrections)

### Limitations
- Maximum 5 users
- Single entity/company
- 10,000 transactions/month
- Maximum 10 payroll employees
- Email support only (48-hour response)
- No API access

### Excluded Features
- Payroll
- Bank reconciliation
- Fixed assets
- ML/AI features
- WORM compliance
- Intercompany transactions

## Tier 4: ProAudit Intelligence (Add-On)

### Pricing
- **Monthly:** ₦250,000 - ₦1,000,000/month
- **Requires:** Professional or Enterprise subscription
- **Annual:** 15% discount available

### Target Market
- Internal audit teams
- Forensic accountants
- Compliance officers (CBN, SEC, NAICOM regulated entities)
- Risk management professionals
- Fraud investigators
- EFCC/ICPC cooperation units

### Features

#### ML-Based Anomaly Detection
- Unsupervised learning models
- Behavioral baseline establishment
- Real-time anomaly scoring
- Configurable sensitivity
- Alert prioritization

#### Benford's Law Analysis
- First-digit distribution analysis
- Second-digit analysis
- Deviation scoring
- Historical trend comparison
- Batch analysis (async processing)

#### Z-Score Statistical Analysis
- Transaction z-score calculation
- Outlier identification
- Peer group comparison
- Time-series analysis
- Configurable thresholds

#### Predictive Forecasting
- Revenue forecasting
- Expense prediction
- Cash flow projections
- Seasonal adjustment
- Confidence intervals

#### NLP Document Processing
- Contract analysis
- Email sentiment analysis
- Memo keyword extraction
- Risk phrase detection
- Multi-language support

#### OCR Invoice Extraction
- Automatic field extraction
- Vendor matching
- Amount verification
- Duplicate detection
- Up to 1,000 pages/month

#### Fraud Pattern Recognition
- Known fraud pattern matching
- Relationship mapping
- Vendor collusion detection
- Ghost employee detection
- Round-number analysis

#### Custom ML Model Training
- Custom model development
- Organization-specific training
- Model performance monitoring
- A/B testing capability
- Model versioning

### Processing Limits
- Benford analysis: 100,000 transactions/run
- Z-Score: 50,000 transactions/run
- OCR: 1,000 pages/month (Professional), 5,000 pages/month (Enterprise)
- ML inference: 10,000 predictions/day

### Important Notes
- All Intelligence features run **asynchronously**
- Large analyses scheduled during off-peak hours
- Results available via dashboard and email notification
- Historical analysis limited to 3 years of data
