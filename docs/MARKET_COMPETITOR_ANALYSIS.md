### 1.2 Tax Compliance Context

The Nigeria Tax Act 2025 introduced transformative changes effective January 1, 2026:

| Change | Impact on Software Market |
|--------|--------------------------|
| **Mandatory E-Invoicing** | Creates immediate demand for NRS-integrated solutions |
| **Progressive PAYE (₦800K tax-free)** | Requires updated payroll systems |
| **Input VAT Recovery Extension** | Creates value proposition for expense tracking |
| **Small Business 0% CIT** | Simplifies compliance for many, but requires accurate turnover tracking |
| **4% Development Levy** | Additional calculation requirement |
