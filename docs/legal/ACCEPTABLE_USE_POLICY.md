# TekVwarho ProAudit - Acceptable Use Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 3, 2026  
**Version:** 1.0  

---

## 1. Introduction

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") outlines the permitted and prohibited uses of TekVwarho ProAudit (the "Service"). This policy is incorporated into and forms part of our Terms and Conditions.

By using the Service, you agree to comply with this AUP. Violation may result in suspension or termination of your account.

---

## 2. Permitted Uses

### 2.1 Authorized Activities

You may use TekVwarho ProAudit to:

- ✅ Record legitimate business income and expenses
- ✅ Generate invoices for actual business transactions
- ✅ Submit compliant e-invoices to NRS
- ✅ Calculate taxes in accordance with Nigerian law
- ✅ Generate financial reports for your business
- ✅ Manage your business inventory
- ✅ Process payroll for your employees
- ✅ Grant authorized access to your accountant or auditor
- ✅ Export data for TaxPro Max filing

### 2.2 Authorized Users

- Business owners and their employees
- Accountants engaged by the business
- Auditors granted read-only access
- Tax consultants acting on behalf of the business

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

### 3.1 Illegal Activities

You must NOT use the Service for:

| Prohibited Activity | Description |
|---------------------|-------------|
| **Tax Evasion** | Creating false records to evade taxes |
| **Fraud** | Generating fraudulent invoices or receipts |
| **Money Laundering** | Processing proceeds of crime |
| **Identity Theft** | Using false business identities |
| **Forgery** | Creating falsified documents |

### 3.2 System Abuse

You must NOT:

- Attempt to access other users' accounts or data
- Probe, scan, or test system vulnerabilities
- Interfere with service availability (DoS attacks)
- Reverse engineer, decompile, or extract source code
- Circumvent security measures or authentication
- Use automated scripts to access the Service (except via authorized API)
- Overload the system with excessive requests

### 3.3 Data Misuse

You must NOT:

- Upload malware, viruses, or harmful code
- Upload content that violates third-party rights
- Collect personal data of others without consent
- Share or sell access credentials
- Use data for unauthorized purposes

### 3.4 False Information

You must NOT:

- Submit false invoices to NRS
- Create fictitious vendors or customers
- Inflate or deflate expenses or income
- Misrepresent business activities
- Provide false registration information

---

## 4. Compliance Requirements

### 4.1 Tax Compliance

Users must:

- Submit only accurate, truthful information to tax authorities
- Maintain proper documentation for all transactions
- Comply with filing deadlines
- Retain records as required by law (6 years)

### 4.2 Data Protection

Users must:

- Process personal data in compliance with NDPA
- Obtain consent for employee/customer data
- Not share confidential information inappropriately
- Report suspected data breaches immediately

---

## 5. Enforcement

### 5.1 Monitoring

We may monitor usage to:

- Ensure compliance with this AUP
- Protect system integrity
- Respond to legal requirements
- Investigate reported violations

### 5.2 Violations

If we determine you have violated this AUP, we may:

| Violation Severity | Action |
|-------------------|--------|
| **Minor (First)** | Warning and guidance |
| **Minor (Repeat)** | Temporary suspension |
| **Major** | Immediate suspension pending investigation |
| **Severe** | Permanent termination and legal referral |

### 5.3 Reporting Violations

To report suspected violations:

Email: abuse@tekvwarho.com

Include:
- Description of the violation
- Evidence (if available)
- Account or user involved (if known)

## 6. Consequences of Violations

### 6.1 Account Actions

- Warning notification
- Feature restrictions
- Temporary suspension
- Permanent termination

### 6.2 Legal Consequences

Serious violations may be reported to:

- Nigeria Revenue Service (NRS)
- Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC)
- Nigeria Police Force (Cybercrime Unit)
- Other relevant authorities

### 6.3 Liability

You are responsible for:

- All activities under your account
- Actions of your authorized users
- Damages resulting from violations
- Legal fees and penalties
