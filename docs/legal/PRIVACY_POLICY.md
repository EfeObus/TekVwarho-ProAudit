## 9. Your Data Rights

Under the Nigeria Data Protection Act 2023, you have the following rights:

| Right | Description | How to Exercise |
|-------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Access** | Request a copy of your personal data | Contact DPO or use in-app export |
| **Rectification** | Correct inaccurate personal data | Edit in app or contact DPO |
| **Erasure** | Request deletion of your data (where legally permitted) | Contact DPO |
| **Restriction** | Limit how we process your data | Contact DPO |
| **Portability** | Receive your data in a machine-readable format | Use in-app export (CSV/Excel) |
| **Objection** | Object to processing based on legitimate interest | Contact DPO |
| **Withdraw Consent** | Withdraw consent for optional processing | Settings or contact DPO |

**Response Time:** We will respond to rights requests within 30 days.

**Complaints:** You may lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission (NDPC) if you believe your rights have been violated.
