## 2. What Are Cookies?

### 2.1 Definition

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile browser) when you visit a website. They are widely used to:
- Make websites work efficiently
- Remember your preferences
- Provide a personalized experience
- Collect analytics data

### 2.2 Types of Storage Technologies

| Technology | Description | Duration |
|------------|-------------|----------|
| **Cookies** | Small text files stored in your browser | Variable (session to years) |
| **Local Storage** | Browser storage for larger data amounts | Until manually cleared |
| **Session Storage** | Temporary storage cleared when browser closes | Session only |
| **Fingerprinting** | We do NOT use browser fingerprinting | N/A |

## 3. Categories of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

These cookies are required for the Service to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Provider |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintains your login session | Session | TekVwarho |
| `csrf_token` | Prevents cross-site request forgery attacks | Session | TekVwarho |
| `entity_context` | Remembers which business entity you're working in | Session | TekVwarho |
| `auth_token` | Authentication token for API requests | 24 hours | TekVwarho |
| `cookie_consent` | Stores your cookie preferences | 1 year | TekVwarho |

**Legal Basis:** Legitimate interest - these are necessary for the Service to operate.

### 3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enhance your experience by remembering your preferences.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Provider |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|
| `user_preferences` | Remembers UI settings (language, theme) | 1 year | TekVwarho |
| `dashboard_layout` | Saves your dashboard customizations | 1 year | TekVwarho |

**Legal Basis:** Consent - you can disable these without affecting core functionality.

### 3.3 Analytics Cookies

These cookies help us understand how users interact with our Service.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration | Provider |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|
| `_pk_id` | Distinguishes unique visitors | 13 months | Matomo (Self-hosted) |
| `_pk_ses` | Stores session data | 30 minutes | Matomo (Self-hosted) |
| `_pk_ref` | Stores referrer information | 6 months | Matomo (Self-hosted) |

**Note:** We use privacy-focused, self-hosted analytics (Matomo) that:
- Does NOT share data with third parties
- Anonymizes IP addresses
- Respects Do Not Track settings
- Stores data on our servers, not third-party servers

**Legal Basis:** Consent - you can opt out of analytics cookies.

## 6. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

### 6.1 Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit our Service, you will see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Accept only essential cookies
- Customize your preferences

### 6.2 Changing Preferences

You can change your cookie preferences at any time by:

**Option 1: In-App Settings**
1. Log into TekVwarho ProAudit
2. Navigate to Settings → Privacy
3. Adjust cookie preferences
4. Click "Save Changes"

**Option 2: Browser Settings**
You can configure your browser to:
- Block all cookies
- Block third-party cookies only
- Delete cookies when you close your browser
- Prompt you before accepting cookies

**Browser-Specific Instructions:**

| Browser | How to Manage Cookies |
|---------|----------------------|
| **Chrome** | Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies |
| **Firefox** | Settings → Privacy & Security → Cookies and Site Data |
| **Safari** | Preferences → Privacy → Manage Website Data |
| **Edge** | Settings → Cookies and Site Permissions |

### 6.3 Impact of Disabling Cookies

| Cookie Type | Impact if Disabled |
|-------------|-------------------|
| **Essential** | Cannot use the Service (login will fail) |
| **Functional** | Preferences won't be remembered |
| **Analytics** | No impact on functionality |

### 6.4 Do Not Track

We respect the "Do Not Track" (DNT) browser setting. If you have DNT enabled:
- Analytics cookies will not be set
- No usage data will be collected
- Core functionality remains unaffected
