Frequently Asked Questions

General

TekVwarho ProAudit is Nigeria's premier tax compliance and business management platform, designed specifically for the 2026 Tax Reform era. It provides automated VAT, PAYE, WHT, and CIT calculations, NRS e-invoicing integration, transaction recording, and comprehensive financial reporting.
Yes! TekVwarho ProAudit is fully compliant with the Nigeria Revenue Service (NRS) e-invoicing requirements. Our platform integrates directly with the FIRS e-Invoice system to generate IRN (Invoice Reference Numbers) and submit invoices electronically.

Tax & Compliance

We support all major Nigerian taxes including:

  • VAT (7.5%): Input/Output VAT tracking and return preparation
  • PAYE: Updated 2026 tax bands (0%, 15%, 20%, 25%, 30%)
  • WHT: Automatic calculation by service type and payee
  • CIT: Company size-based rates (0%, 20%, 30%)
Once you finalize an invoice, simply click the "Submit to NRS" button. ProAudit will automatically submit the invoice to FIRS and retrieve the Invoice Reference Number (IRN). The IRN and QR code will be added to your invoice for compliance.
