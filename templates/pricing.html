Yes! You can upgrade your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades will be applied at the end of your current billing period.

Yes! All new accounts start with a 14-day free trial of the Core plan with full access to all features. No credit card required.

We accept payments via bank transfer, debit cards, and Paystack. For Enterprise plans, we also offer invoicing with NET-30 terms.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! You save 20% when you choose annual billing. That's like getting 2+ months free.
